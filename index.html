Transparent On-chain Utility + Charity Based DeFi Token
Charity Chain (CHC) creates continuous, transparent funding for real-world charitable activities while offering holders token utility. Every mechanism and payout is recorded on-chain and published with supporting proof.

Charity Chain was created to bring accountability and automation to charitable giving. Instead of one-off campaigns, CHC channels a small portion of tokenomics and staking yield to a public funding wallet that finances food distribution, community kitchens, medical help and micro-business grants.

Funds are never moved without on-chain transparency. Each major disbursement is accompanied by transaction hashes, receipts and short video documentation allowing donors and the public to independently verify outcomes.

We combine modern DeFi tools with traditional philanthropic principles to deliver a continuous sadaqah model that benefits vulnerable communities globally. Our operations prioritize local partners and measurable impact.

CHC is deployed on Polygon for low-fee transactions and wide accessibility, making it easy for donors worldwide to participate and verify outcomes.

Our contract addresses, team actions and social records are public and auditable—this is charity you can watch work in real time.
